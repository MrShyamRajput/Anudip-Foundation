Motor Vehicles in the Indian Market

Scooter

A scooter is a two-wheeled vehicle that is lightweight, easy to ride, and ideal for short-distance travel.
It is fuel-efficient and commonly used in cities for its convenience and affordability.

MotorCycle

A motorcycle is a two-wheeled motor vehicle designed for speed, agility, and long-distance travel.
It is widely used for personal transport and adventure riding, offering powerful performance and fuel efficiency.

Hatcgback

A hatchback is a compact car with a rear door that opens upwards, providing access to the cargo area. It is popular for its practicality, fuel efficiency, and ease of driving in urban areas.

Sedan

A sedan is a four-door passenger car with a separate trunk for luggage storage. It offers a comfortable ride, spacious interiors, and is ideal for families and long-distance travel.

SUV (Sport Utility Vehicle)

An SUV (Sport Utility Vehicle) is a powerful and spacious vehicle designed for both city driving and off-road adventures. It features high ground clearance, strong build, and is ideal for families, travel, and rough terrain.

MUV (Multi Utility Vehicle)

An MUV (Multi Utility Vehicle) is a versatile vehicle designed to carry more passengers and cargo with ease. It is ideal for large families or commercial use, offering spacious interiors and comfortable seating.

Electric Vehicle (EV)

An Electric Vehicle (EV) runs on electricity instead of petrol or diesel, making it eco-friendly and cost-efficient. EVs produce zero tailpipe emissions and are powered by rechargeable batteries, offering a cleaner alternative for transportation.
